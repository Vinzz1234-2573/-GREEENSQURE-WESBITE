// Who We Are

Sustainability
starts with you.

Green Square is Singapore's leading textile recycling platform — connecting residents, schools, and businesses with a circular economy for clothing.

GS

// Our Mission

Doubling the textile recycling rate by 2030.

Singapore generates over 150,000 tonnes of textile waste every year. Yet only 7% is recycled — the rest ends up in Semakau Landfill, which is on track to run out of space by 2035.

Green Square was founded on one conviction: that number can and must change. Our target is to drive Singapore's textile recycling rate from 7% to 14% by 2030 — a modest goal with an enormous impact if communities, companies, and schools choose to act together.

Every piece of clothing donated isn't just fabric saved. It's water conserved, carbon avoided, and a signal that a circular economy is possible here.

// Our Philosophy

Three Rs. One planet.

We don't believe in a throwaway culture. Everything has a second life — we just need to find it.

01
REUSE

Wearable clothes, paired shoes, and accessories go directly to second-hand markets, donation drives, and social enterprises — worn again by someone who needs them.

02
REPURPOSE

Items too worn for rewearing are shredded into industrial wiping rags — sold to factories across Singapore. Even your worst old t-shirt has a job to do.

03
REPAIR

We partner with tailors and repair workshops to give lightly damaged items a new lease of life before they ever reach the recycling stage.

// Our Journey

Building a circular
economy, step by step.

2017

Greensquare Pte Ltd incorporated (UEN: 201731590G). First textile collection bin deployed in Bishan.

2018

NEA Recognition & First School Programmes

Approved by the National Environment Agency. Rolled out our first 3Rs education workshops in Singapore primary schools.

2020

Corporate Partnerships Begin

DBS, KPMG, and Netflix join as CSR partners. Green Square runs its first large-scale corporate textile drives.

2022

100+ Drop-Off Points

Collection network reaches over 100 bins island-wide. Grab, Visa, and Singapore Dance Theatre join the partner network.

2024

850+ Tonnes Collected

250+ drop-off points. Over 1.2 million items given a second life. 650,000 kg kept from Semakau Landfill.

2030

14% Recycling Rate — The Goal

Double Singapore's current textile recycling rate. With your support, we can get there.
