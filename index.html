// 03.5 · WHAT WE ACCEPT

What can you
give us?

Almost everything textile. Hover each category to find out — if in doubt, bring it in.

👗
Clothing

Clean tops, bottoms, dresses, outerwear, and formalwear. Any brand or size — as long as it's clean.

✓ Accepted
👟
Paired Shoes

Wearable shoes, sandals, and boots. Must be in wearable condition and come as a matched pair.

✓ Accepted
🛏️
Household Linen

Clean bed sheets, pillowcases, and towels. Dry and clean before donating.

✓ Accepted
👜
Accessories

Belts and bags in reasonable condition. Leather and fabric both welcome.

✓ Accepted
👔
Uniforms

Corporate, school, and workwear uniforms. We have dedicated uniform recycling programmes with hospitals and corporates.

✓ Accepted
👗
Household Clothes

Clothing donations for the household — all family members, all ages.

✓ Accepted
🧸
Soft Toys & Pillows

We do not accept soft toys, pillows, comforters, or fabric scraps. Please check with NEA for bulky or household item disposal.

✗ Not accepted
🚫
Dirty / Damaged Items

Stained, torn, moldy, wet, or used undergarments and worn socks are not accepted. Items must be clean and dry.

✗ Not accepted

// 09 · THE PROCESS

What happens
to your clothes?

01

Collected

Collected from our 250+ drop-off bins across Singapore, sealed and transported to our sorting hub — no clothing touches landfill at this stage.

02

Processed

Trained sorters assess every item. Wearable clothes are baled for second-hand retail or donated via our NEA-approved network.

03

Downcycled

Torn, stained, or worn items are shredded into industrial wiping rags — sold to factories across Singapore as a circular product.
