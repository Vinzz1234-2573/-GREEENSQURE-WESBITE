// What We Collect

Bring us
almost anything.

From everyday clothes to school uniforms to household linen — if it's clean and textile, we'll find it a second life.

YES

The golden rule: If it's clean, dry, and made of fabric — bring it in. When in doubt, just drop it off.We'll sort it out.

// Accepted Items

What we gladly take.

👗
Clothing — All Types

Tops, bottoms, dresses, outerwear, and formalwear. Men's, women's, and children's clothing. Any brand, any size — as long as it's clean and dry.

✓ Accepted
👟
Paired Shoes

Wearable shoes, sandals, sneakers, and boots. Must be in wearable condition and come as a matched pair. Tie them together before dropping off.

✓ Accepted
🛏️
Household Linen

Clean bed sheets, pillowcases, duvet covers, and towels. Please ensure they are washed and completely dry before donating.

✓ Accepted
👜
Bags & Accessories

Handbags, backpacks, and belts in reasonable condition. Leather and fabric both welcome. Ensure they are clean and dry.

✓ Accepted
👔
Uniforms

Corporate, school, hospital, and workwear uniforms. We have dedicated uniform recycling programmes for large organisations — contact us for bulk collection.

✓ Accepted
🧥
Coats & Jackets

Winter coats, rain jackets, blazers, and hoodies. Clean and dry. Even if they're out of style — someone will find a use for them.

✓ Accepted

// Not Accepted

What we cannot take.

These items cannot be recycled through Green Square's network. For bulky items, contact NEA for special disposal options.

🧸
Soft Toys & Stuffed Animals

We do not accept soft toys or stuffed animals. Contact NEA or local shelters for donations.

✗ Not accepted
🛋️
Pillows & Comforters

Pillows, bolsters, and comforters cannot be processed through our textile recycling network.

✗ Not accepted
✂️
Fabric Scraps

Loose fabric pieces, yarn, and off-cuts from sewing cannot be accepted at drop-off bins.

✗ Not accepted
🩲
Used Undergarments

Used undergarments and worn socks are not accepted for hygiene reasons.

✗ Not accepted
🚫
Dirty or Wet Items

Stained, torn, mouldy, or wet items contaminate other donations. Please only donate clean, dry textiles.

✗ Not accepted
📚
Books & Non-Textiles

Books, kitchenware, electronics, and other household items cannot be placed in Green Square bins.

✗ Not accepted

// Preparation Tips

Before you drop off.

A few simple steps help us process your donation faster and ensure more items can be reused.

01
Clean & Dry

Wash clothes and dry them completely. Mouldy or wet donations cannot be accepted and contaminate other items.

02
Bag It Up

Place items in a clean bag (reusable or plastic). This keeps donations clean and makes collection easier for our team.

03
Pair Your Shoes

Tie shoes together or place each pair in a small bag. Unpaired shoes cannot be matched and are harder to donate.

04
When in Doubt

If you're unsure, bring it to us anyway. Our team will sort it — the worst outcome is we downcycle it responsibly.
