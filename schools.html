// What We Offer

Two programmes.
One mission.

Green Square school programmes are fully free and run by our team. All you need to do is set a date and promote it to your school community.

♻️
Textile Recycling Drive

We install collection bins at your school for 1–3 weeks. Students and staff bring in clean clothes, shoes, and accessories. Our team collects and processes everything at the end of the drive.

📚
3Rs Education Workshop

An interactive 45–60 minute session for students (Primary 3 to Secondary 4) on Singapore's textile waste challenge, the 3Rs, and what happens to donated clothes. Curriculum-aligned and hands-on.

🏆
School Impact Report

After every drive, your school receives a full impact certificate and report — total weight collected, items reused, environmental impact in CO₂ and water saved. Perfect for SLT presentations and EcoSchool programmes.

// Why It Matters

What students learn
about textile waste.

Singapore sends over 150,000 tonnes of textile waste to landfill every year — only 7% is recycled.

Growing cotton for one pair of jeans uses approximately 6,813 litres of water.

The blue HDB recycling bins are not designed for textiles — clothes there end up in landfill.

Semakau Landfill is projected to reach capacity by 2035 — textile recycling can help extend its life.

7%

Current textile recycling rate in Singapore.
Our goal: 14% by 2030.
