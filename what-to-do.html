// How It Works

Five steps.
Zero to landfill.

01

Drop Off

Clean, bagged clothes into any Green Square bin near your HDB block or CC.

02

We Collect

Our team empties bins regularly and transports textiles to our sorting hub.

03

Sort & Grade

Every item inspected and sorted by condition and material type.

04

Reuse or Repurpose

Wearables go to second-hand markets, donation drives, or social enterprises.

05

Impact Tracked

Every kg logged. Full transparency so you know where your clothes went.

// The Process

What happens
to your clothes?

01

Collected

Collected from our 250+ drop-off bins across Singapore, sealed and transported to our sorting hub — no clothing touches landfill at this stage.

📦
02

Processed

Trained sorters assess every item. Wearable clothes are baled for second-hand retail or donated via our NEA-approved network.

🔍
03

Downcycled

Torn, stained, or worn items are shredded into industrial wiping rags — sold to factories across Singapore as a circular product.

⚙️

Know what
we accept?

Almost everything textile — clothing, shoes, linen, accessories, and uniforms. Find out exactly what's in and what's out before you bag everything up.
