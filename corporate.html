// How It Works

Four steps to your
company's textile drive.

We keep it simple so your team can focus on what matters — getting people to donate.

01

Enquire

Fill in the form below or email us. We'll reply within 2 working days with a proposal tailored to your company size and goals.

02

We Set Up

Our team delivers bins, collection bags, and branded signage. We brief your champion on how to promote the drive internally.

03

You Collect

Run the drive for 1–4 weeks. Employees bring in clean clothing, shoes, and accessories. We're available for any questions throughout.

04

Impact Report

We collect everything, process it, and send you a detailed impact report within 7 working days. Perfect for ESG and CSR documentation.
