// Why Run a Drive

Benefits for your
MCST and residents.

A textile recycling event is a meaningful, easy win for your estate's sustainability programme — and residents love it. Here's what you get.

🏘️
Community Building

A drive gives residents a shared sustainability goal. It builds community spirit and demonstrates that your management committee cares about more than maintenance.

📦
Zero Admin for MCST

We deliver, install, and collect everything. Your team doesn't lift a finger — other than sending one newsletter to residents.

🌿
Sustainability Report

Receive a full impact certificate after the event — total kg collected, CO₂ saved, and items reused. Share it in your AGM report or residents' newsletter.

🔄
Ongoing Bin Option

Want year-round impact? Install a permanent Green Square bin in your lobby or carpark. We maintain and empty it regularly — no cost to your estate.

// How It Works

Four simple steps
for your estate.

The whole process typically takes 3–4 weeks from enquiry to impact report.

01

Enquire

Fill in the form below. We'll contact you within 2 working days to discuss your estate's size, goals, and preferred dates.

02

We Set Up

Our team delivers collection bins, branded bags, and resident-facing signage. We brief you on how to promote the drive.

03

Residents Donate

Run the drive for 1–3 weeks. Residents drop off clean clothes, shoes, and accessories. No sorting required from your team.

04

Impact Report

We collect everything and send your estate's impact certificate within 7 days. Share it proudly with your residents.
